# Ira Bukht — Google Ads prompts for SaaS accounts

## Extracted Content
This thread explains five prompts for auditing and improving Google Ads accounts: wasted search terms, broken tracking, creeping CPA, mismatched GA4/Ads conversions, and top keywords eating budget without return.

## Key Takeaways
- check search terms with spend but zero conversions
- run regular full-account audits
- track CPA week over week, not just point-in-time
- compare Google Ads conversions with GA4
- inspect top-spend keywords and the search terms actually triggering them
